Business Trip Management

"Easy Business Trip and its expenses Management"

This module helps an organization to better manage Business trip requests and expense management.

Description

This module helps an organization to better manage business trip requests and expense management. Managing business travel shouldn’t be complicated so the purpose of the Business Trip module is to manage business trip dynamics and the respective costs. The business trip module has highly configurable workflow engine where almost no real coding is required to design any complicated process.

Key Features

  • Any employee can create a business trip request.

  • Managing complicated workflow approvals, based on organization policies.

  • Multilevel configurable approval and rejection notification.

  • Fully integrated with finance to generate expenses.

  • Configurable allowances by geographic location.

  • Ability to register all business trip expenses and cost related.

  • Ability to track expenses with different dimensions for example (employees, countries, departments, job positions, trip titles, ticket class etc.).
