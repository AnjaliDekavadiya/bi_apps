Sale Order and Purchase Order History Product Order Lines in Odoo

Sales Order Line History (Last 10 Sales Order Lines)

Purchase Order Line History (Last 10 Purchase Order Lines)

This app allows your users to see sales order and purchase order history lines for the last 10 orders for that customer and vendor.

Main Features:

  • Added History Sales Order Lines in Sale Order Form View and Sales Manager can see History Order Lines.
  • Add History Order Lines :- Add last 10 latest sale orders related to Customer and their Company Contacts, which state is Sales Order or Locked (sale or done), when creating new sales order and fill-up Customer field. You have to press compute button to see last 10 order lines for every product in sales order lines.
  • Added History Order Lines in Purchase Order Form View.
  • Add History Order Lines :- Add last 10 latest purchase orders related to Vendor and their Company Contacts, which state is Purchase Order or Locked (purchase or done), when creating new purchase order and fill-up Vendor field. You have to press compute button to see last 10 order lines for every product in purchase order lines.
  • Last Sales Order History Lines and Last Purchase Order History Lines.
