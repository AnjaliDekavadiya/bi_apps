Cash On Delivery on Website Ecommerce Shop

Payment Acquire Cash on Delivery (COD) in Odoo

This app allows your customers to choose Cash on Delivery Option on Odoo Shop Payment.

Main Features

  • Allow you to configure Minimum Order Amount and Maximum Order Amount on COD Payment Acquire.
  • Allow you to configure Not Allowed Product on Cash on Delivery on COD Payment Acquire.
  • Allow you to configure States where Cash on Delivery Available on Payment Acquires.
  • Allow you to configure ZIP Where Cash on Delivery Available on Payment Acquire.
  • If any conditions do not match then the customer will not be allowed to choose COD option.
  • Cash on delivery collection form and list view and print pdf.
  • For more details you can watch the video and check screenshots below.

Minimum and Maximum Order Amount Limit

You can set limit here and system will see if order is come into this limit then only it will allow to proceed with Cash On Delivery payment option to your customer.

Cash On Delivery Payment Acquire - Countries Filter (Odoo Standard)

This countries filter will hide Cash On Delivery if your customer does not belongs to countries listed here.

Product Restriction on Cash On Delivery Payment Acquire

Product listed here will not be applicable for Cash On Delivery on website shop. If you customer has choosen any product from this list then customer can not use Cash On Delivery option.

Cash On Delivery Payment Acquire - States Filter

This states filter will hide Cash On Delivery if your customer does not belongs to states listed here.

Cash On Delivery Payment Acquire - Zip Code Filter

This Zip code filter will hide Cash On Delivery if your customer does not belongs to zip codes listed here.
