Odoo Marketplace Rental Sale

Now offer products on Rent in your Odoo Marketplace!

Odoo Marketplace Rental Sale allows you to offer products on rent in your Odoo Marketplace. The module lets you manage the rental products of your sellers and publish them on your online store.

Trend of Rental Services among People

Rental services are always on trend; especially, among the millennials as they stay far from their homes. Rental services are really useful when people want to avail any product for a particular time period. Hence, they need to pay for the time they have used.

People out for their work or studies prefer to rent products instead of buying as renting is affordable as well as they can only take it for the particular time defined.

Whether it is bed to the AC's; from vehicles to the furniture of a house one can rent anything. Basically, renting as an option for those who don't want to buy things for any reason.

Manage Rental Services in Odoo Marketplace

Now, you can offer rental services to your customers or allow your sellers to rent out their products and charge for the products on the basis of a particular time period. Odoo Marketplace Rental Sale allows you to manage rent services and offer them hassle-freely.

You can also manage the contract and agreement of the rental product as per the requirements. You as an admin, have full authority to approve or disapprove any rental product.

Your customers can easily visit the frontend and select any rental product to avail and simply pay for the tenure they wish. Hence, you can easily manage rental services and move with the trend for the boost of your business.

Manage the Rent Tenure as per Customer's Flexibility

You or your sellers can set some standard rent tenure along with the price so that your customers can pick anyone as per their need. However, some of the customers may need the product for the time period that is not defined by you.

Offer Flexibility to your customers, now they can not only select from the standard tenure but also customize the rental tenure as they require.

Moreover, you can show the security amount of the rental product to the customers for transparency which builds a relation of trust among customers. It lets the customers choose you again whenever they require to rent a product.

Detailed Features List

Create Rental Products in Odoo Marketplace

  • You/sellers can make any product available for rent
  • Once enabled the rental option, you can configure it for renting

Rental Product Management in Odoo Marketplace

  • Sellers can create/manage only their rental products
  • As an admin you can create and approve or disapprove any rental product

Manage the Rental Product Agreement

  • You can manage the rental product agreement from Odoo backend
  • You can create multiple agreements and select anyone for a product

Add the Security Amount of the Product

  • You can add the security amount in a rental product
  • The security amount is displayed in the product page of the front-end
  • You can also set Rental Tenure Starting in the product

Set the Rental Tenure Type

  • You can add standard tenure for product rental
  • Standard tenure comprises all the details predefined
  • Custom tenure comprises the UOM of tenure
  • Customer can select UOM and set tenure as they want under custom tenure
