Detailed Features List

Add a payment method for subscription products

  • The module adds the functionality to make payment of subscription-based services/products using Paypal payment method.
  • PayPal Express Checkout Payment shows up as one of the payment methods on the check out page of subscription-based services/products.

Fast, Secure and Hassle-free payment

  • The customers can complete the payment on your website without being redirected to other websites.
  • The customers can choose to log in to their PayPal account or pay as a guest on the PayPal payment portal.
  • Use PayPal balance or any saved debit/credit cards to make the payment
  • The customer needs to fill in the required details to complete the transaction.

Track Transaction History of Subscription Products/Services

  • The Odoo admin can see the transaction details of the orders in the Odoo backend.
  • The Subscription form shows the details of the mode of payment used by the customer to make the payment for the order.
  • The list of the transaction is also maintained in the PayPal merchant portal of the odoo admin.

Trial Period for the Subscription Products

  • The Odoo admin can also set trial periods for subscription-based products.
  • The trial periods can be set on the basis of the products and trail on one time, all is upto the admin.
  • The invoice cannot be created for a subscription plan which is in the trial period.
