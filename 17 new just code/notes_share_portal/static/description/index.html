Share Notes on Portal Website Odoo app.

Share Notes to Customers / Vendors / Contacts / External User on Portal.

This app allows you to share notes on the portal my account of your website.

Main Features:

  • Allow you to share notes to customers/contacts/vendors/partners to the portal of your website.
  • You can configure a share portal field on note form in the backend and then that note will be shared to selected persons.
  • They can view notes on the portal (can not edit) but they can send messages on the portal using the chatter given.
  • For more details please see below screenshots and watch the video.
