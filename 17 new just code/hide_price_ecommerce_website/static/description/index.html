Product Shop Hide Price on Shop Website in Odoo

Call for Price Feature in Odoo

This app will hide product price and shopping cart buttons from website shop ecommerce as shown in below screenshots.

Main Features:

  • Allow you to hide prices from website shop ecommerce for customers and visitors of your website shop.
  • Hide price will work for multi websites based on configuration on website settings.
  • Hide price from product shop page and prices will be hidden for customers and guest/public users.
  • Hide price from website shop product kanban / grid product view.
  • Hide shopping cart button from product kanban box.
  • Hide price from website shop product list view.
  • Hide shopping cart button from product list view
  • Hide price and shopping cart button form website product page.
  • Hide price and shopping cart button from wishlist product list.
  • Hide price and shopping cart button from compare product list.
  • Hide price from search input box dropdown list.
  • Only internal employees with the group "Website Shop Show Price" assigned can see prices on product form on website shops.
  • Get Information for contact person from *Call for price* Button on product shopping page.
  • For more details please see below screenshots and watch the video.
