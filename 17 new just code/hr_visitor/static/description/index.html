Company Visitor Pass for Human Resource Team

Visitor Pass / Guest Pass by Human Resource

Company Visitor Record Management in Odoo

This app allows your HR users to manage company visitors Pass & Details.


Main Features :
  • Allow your HR Users to create / generate the visitor pass and maintain details of company visitors as record in Odoo.
  • Employees / Users can see visitor details only for their own visitors while the HR team can see all visitors records.
