PayLater Payment Acquirer

Odoo PayLater Payment Acquirer is the base module For Odoo Website PayLater. It provides the basic structure to enable Pay Later for your customers.

User Guide: https://webkul.com/blog/user-guide-for-odoo-payment-acquirer-paylater/

You would also need to purchase Odoo website Pay Later with this module for the features to work properly.

  • Odoo PayLater Payment Acquirer acts as payment acquires for Odoo Website Pay Later Module.
  • Increase conversions and reduce shopping cart abandonment on your Odoo website with Pay Later or Future Pay feature.
  • It allows the customers to purchase the products without making instant payments or opting for EMIs.
  • The payments for the orders can be made at a later date set by the Odoo admin.

Odoo Website Pay Later

  • It Works in conjunction with Odoo PayLater Payment Acquirer
  • Facilitates 'Buy Now Pay Later' or commonly known as FuturePay in your Odoo.
  • So, your Customers do not have to worry about making instant payments for their orders.
  • Assign advance Purchase limit to your eligible customers.
  • Set the Date for payment and subsequent penalties.
