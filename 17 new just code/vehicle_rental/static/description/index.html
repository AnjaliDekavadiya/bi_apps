Features

Stats

The dashboard displays vital data: total vehicle count, available vehicles, vehicles under maintenance, total rental contracts, contracts in progress, returned contracts, and canceled contracts.

Vehicle details

You can manage vehicle rents, extra charges per vehicle, and track the status of available vehicles and those under maintenance.

Customer details

You can handle customer details for vehicle rentals, including phone numbers, email addresses, pick-up and drop-off locations, as well as the respective dates.

Rent details

Multiple rent types (days, kilometers, miles) are available, and all calculations are automatically performed based on the chosen rent type.

Vehicle images

You can add multiple images of rented vehicles to easily identify the correct vehicle.

Insurance policy

You can select multiple insurance policies related to vehicle rentals.

Damage and Lost invoice

After the vehicle contract is over, if there are any damages or losses, you can capture vehicle damage images and generate a damage invoice.

Cancellation policy

At any time during the vehicle rental process, you can cancel the vehicle contract and generate a cancellation invoice.

Extra services

You have the option to add extra services to the vehicle rental, and the charge for these services is based on the selected service.

Contract reports

You can generate PDF reports containing the details of the vehicle rental contract.
