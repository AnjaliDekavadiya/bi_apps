Let your Customers View the Products from all Angles

Now, let your sellers add sequential images in the products so that the customers can view the products from all angles. Odoo Marketplace Product 360° View allows customers to have a better look at the product they wish to buy.

Show Product 360° View to Increase The Credibility

Visual marketing is an essential piece to building a brand in this digitized world. Showing the product from all angles to the customers increases the credibility.
According to a study by Shopify, only 0.52% want to see a single product photo, a 33.16% prefer to see multiple photos; and the majority of them, exactly a 58.03%, prefer photographs that will enable them to have a 360º vision of the product.

View Product From All Angles Manually/Automatically

It's your customers’ call to see the product view automatically or drag with the cursor in the direction they want to see.

Decreased Product Returns Rate

Showing 360° Product View allows your customers to review the product completely from all angles.Hence, the customer buys the product after getting complete satisfaction of its look which automatically decreases product return rate.
