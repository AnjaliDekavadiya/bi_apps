Medical Nursing

Used to maintain patients Ambulatory Care details like procedures done , Temperature , heart rate , Respiratory rate , medication and vaccines provided etc.

Patient Rounding details like Six P's , Vital Signs , Diuresis , Glycemia etc. can also be maintained.

Rounding evaluations details like GCS , Pupil Dilation , FiO2 , PEEP , SCE , Oral Mucosa Lesion , Chest Expansion details , Cardiovascular details , Blood and skin evaluations like Bacterimia , Surgery site infection , Wound Dehiscence , Necrotizing Fasciitis etc , Digestive and Abdomen evaluation etc can be done.
