Odoo Marketplace Shipping Per Product

Make shipping better with Odoo Marketplace Shipping per product!

Marketplace Shipping per product helps you to deliver each product separately in the same order. You can choose a different shipping method for each product in the sales order. This module feature can be used from both the backend and front so that customers can choose their preferred delivery method for each product in their order.

Need of Marketplace Shipping Per Product

The marketplace shipping per product module developed by Webkul lets you add different shipping methods for each product in same order. It has been usually seen customers are held back in online shopping due to equal shipping rates for all products, and sometimes charges are too much more than expected. So to solve the issue, Odoo Marketplace Shipping Per Product allows sellers to add delivery methods for their products. Also, it increases your customer reach, which increases your sales too!!

Perks of Marketplace Shipping Per Product

  • The admin and seller can add shipping methods on each product.
  • Admin can also add shipping methods while creating the quotation from the backend.
  • Admin can add shipping methods and their rates from the Odoo backend. Similarly, the customer can add shipping methods for each product in an order on the Odoo website.
  • Sellers have only access to add shipping methods on their approved products.
  • With this module, the delivery price will also be calculated for each product separately.

Why choose shipping per Product module

  • Easy to use and install.
  • Easy to configure.
  • Transparent shipping rates at the front end with the shipping method.
  • Also set individual shipping rates to charge per product.
  • Enable shipping per product based on specific countries.

Detailed Features List

Below is the detailed list of Feature for Odoo Marketplace Shipping Per Product

Allows adding Shipping Methods on products separately

  • This module provides the feature to deliver each product separately in order.
  • The admin can enable per-product shipping on Odoo Marketplace for sellers and customers.

Works for both Odoo backend and frontend

  • Admin can use the feature while placing orders from the backend.
  • The customers can choose per product shipping on the Marketplace website for their online orders.

Add different shipping for a different product in the same order

  • It allows you to select separate shipping for each product in an order.
  • The shipping can be added without breaking up the original order.
  • The total shipping prices are the sum of all the delivery methods selected for products.

Splits delivery in the same order as per selected shipping methods

  • Generates a different delivery for each shipping method selected in the same order.
  • Makes the tracking of delivery much easier than before in Odoo.

Introduces New Shipping Method - "Dynamic Shipping Charges" for admin

  • We have added a new delivery method to handle different shipping methods in same order.
  • Dynamic shipping charges allow you to spilt shipping per product in the order in the backend.
  • The sum of shipping charges of each delivery method is shown in the order total.

Configuration Settings for creating Shipping methods from the backend.

Create new shipping methods by adding a name, provider, margin, company, and website.

Adding shipping method/cost on the quotation from the backend.

Adding new Shipping methods only on approved products

Choosing the shipping method from the provided one during the checkout
