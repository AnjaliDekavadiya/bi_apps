Signature of Customer on Helpdesk Support Ticket

Customer Signature / Sign on Ticket in Odoo

This module allows your customers to sign on a helpdesk ticket on the portal of your website.


Main Features:

  • Allow your customer to sign on a ticket & view sign on ticket form on frontend.
  • Your support team can send signature requests from the ticket form in the backend.
  • Customers will get mail for signature requests and can do signatures on tickets on the website portal.
  • Once a customer signs on a ticket they will get a response and thank you mail from the system.
  • Customers can view signatures on portal website.
  • This module is only compatible with Odoo community edition.
  • For more details watch video and check below screenshots.
