Helpdesk Support Ticket Reassign/Escalate Team and Responsible

This App only Support Odoo Community Edition

This app allows you to re-assign/Escalate support tickets to a responsible user and team and notify them by emails.


Main Features:

  • Allow you to re-assign tickets to the user and notify them by email.
  • Allow you to re-assign tickets to the team and notify Team Leader by email.
  • For more details please watch the video and check below screenshots.

Edition Compatibility:


Only Community

Helpdesk Support Ticket Re-assign Wizard

Click a Reassign Ticket button to open a wizard.

Helpdesk Support Ticket Reassign PopUp

It will set a new user or team to a ticket and notify the user for his new ticket based on the selected option on popup.

Email Notifications

User is notified by email when he/she is assigned on a ticket.

Email Notifications

Team Leader is notified by email when his team is assigned on ticket.
