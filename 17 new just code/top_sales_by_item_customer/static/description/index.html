Top Products and Top Customers of Month in Odoo App

This app allows sales managers to view Top Selling Items and Top Customers of Month in PDF format as shown.

Main Features
  • This app provides a feature to print pdf reports to show top selling items/products and top selling customers of the month for sales order.
  • You are allowed to enter a wizard number of products and number of customers you want to see in the top list.
  • For more details please watch the video and check below screenshots.

Menus Available:

    • Reporting
      • Top Selling Products
      • Top Selling Customers
