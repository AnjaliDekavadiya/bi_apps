Receive Order by Email - By Customer

This app allows your customers to receive orders by email with a PDF of orders as shown in the below screenshots.

Main Features:
  • Send by email button on Payment Information / Confirmation Order Page of Website Shop of Odoo standard as shown.
  • Customers can receive orders in multiple emails using comma-separated.
  • Send by email button is also available in the My Account portal of your website under Sales Orders/ Quotation on Portal of Odoo standard.
  • Button to get email in order available in two places:
    • 1. Payment confirmation page of Odoo standard when a customer completes his/her order on the website shop.
    • 2. My Account portal side at Sales Quotation / Order portal view.
