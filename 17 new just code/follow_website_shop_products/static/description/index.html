Follow and UnFollow Website Ecommerce Product by Customer in Odoo

Follow Product on Website in Odoo Shop

This module allows your customers to follow the product on your website shop.


Main Features :
  • Customers follow the product on the website shop.
  • System will create customer follow history in the backend.
  • Allow your sales team to send messages to customers who are following the product.
  • Allow customers to unfollow products anytime from the product page.
  • For more details please watch the video and check below screenshots.
