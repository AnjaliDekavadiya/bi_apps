Odoo Office 365 Connector

Ksolves Office 365 Connectors Work with all the applications that you have been using all these years along with the benefits of cloud. Streamline all your office tasks and business processes efficiently and conveniently by integrating them with Odoo Office 365. Get the best-in-class productivity by the two-way connection between people and information.

Highlights

Access Anything, Anywhere

Better collaboration with customers and staff, results in better productivity and overall success of the business.

Work on the go

You can work from anywhere you want to, share files, docs, images, etc., on any device you choose and stay connected with your team throughout.

Stay updated

Now do not get distracted with updates, sync, and installation notifications while working. Odoo Office365 will manage everything at the backend and you will always stay updated with the latest versions of all the applications

Don’t lose connectivity, wherever you are

With Odoo Office365, you can access emails, shared contacts from anywhere and stay in sync always.

Bi-directional Syncing

With Odoo Office365, you can sync Odoo contacts to Office365 and vice versa. With just one click, you can sort the Odoo and Office365 data properly.

Hassle-free scheduling

With Odoo Office365, syncing emails, and contact information is no longer a troublesome task. Scheduling is easy, hassle-free and on-the-go.
