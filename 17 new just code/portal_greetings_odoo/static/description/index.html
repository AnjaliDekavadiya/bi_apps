Greeting Message to Customer / Vendor on Portal of Website Odoo

Show Greetings to Portal Customer in Odoo in My Account Menu of Website

This app shows a greeting message to your customer / vendor / portal user when they logged in to the portal of the website under My Account menu.

Main Features :
  • Show Good Morning , Good Afternoon, Good Evening Greetings to customers / portal users.
  • Greetings of Morning, Afternoon, Evening will be based on the timezone set on your customer / portal user form. You can also translate it in your customer language by creating PO files in our module.
  • For more details please watch the video and check below screenshots.

Edition Compatibility:
