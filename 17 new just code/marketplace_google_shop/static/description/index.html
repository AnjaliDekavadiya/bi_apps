Now, integrate your Odoo Marketplace with Google shop!

Odoo Marketplace Google Shop allows you to integrate Google Merchant Account with your Odoo Marketplace. On using this module you can verify your Odoo store domain with Google. You can now sync and store your Odoo product data to your Google merchant account and easily create the product feeds for Google Ads campaign.

Prerequisites

To configure the module successfully, you would need the following:

1) A Google Developer Console account. This account is needed to obtain client Id and client secret for the configuration.
Click on the following link to create a Google Developer console account >> https://console.developers.google.com/

2) A Google Merchant Account to get a merchant ID. Google merchant account is also needed to sync your and upload your product details from Odoo.
CLICK HERE (https://support.google.com/merchants/answer/188924?hl=en) to create a new account.
Then paste the merchant ID in Odoo under the account menu.
