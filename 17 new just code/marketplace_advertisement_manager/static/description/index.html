Give Space to your sellers/customers to create ads in Odoo Marketplace!

Now, you can allow your sellers and customers to create and publish their advertisements on your Odoo marketplace. Basically, the customers/sellers buy out the extra white space for their ads. As an admin, you get all the rights to unpublish any ad anytime.

Your customers/sellers just need to pay out the sum for the number of days they want to run their ad on your marketplace.

Benefits of Odoo Marketplace Advertisement Manager for you

Apart from running a very own marketplace, you can earn extra profit from your not so used spaces. Now, sell out the extra spaces of your marketplace on the price you decide. Hence, you will get extra money from the extra space of your online store.

It is a great marketing strategy to engage more customers as well as sellers to your online store.So, you will get the monetary benefits along with better customer engagement.

Benefits of Odoo Marketplace Advertisement Manager for your Customers/Sellers

The module allows your sellers/customers to create and display their advertisements on your online store. Hence, it offers them a chance to steal the attention of the visitors of your marketplace and eventually convince them to go for their products and services.

The customers always prefer to buy the products that catch their attention first; hence, your sellers can grab their spaces.

Basically, it will help your sellers to increase their sale directly.

Detailed Features List

Here is the detailed list of features for Odoo Marketplace Advertisement Manager

Sell the Spaces for Advertisements

  • Admin offers space to sellers/customers for their advertisements
  • You can set a price per day for every advertisement position.

Set Auto Approval of Ad blocs

  • Admin can globally set the auto approval of ad blocks.

Put the Active Duration of the Advertisement

  • A date range can be selected for which advertisement will be displayed and
  • Customers/sellers can pay as per the number of days they want to run their ad.

Sellers & customers can purchase the Ad display

  • Sellers and customers can purchase the advertisement display positions as per the availability.
  • Customers can only add Banners on the advertisement position booked.
  • Sellers can add banners as products as per their choice on the advertisement position booked.

Adding Products for Advertisements

  • Sellers can add only their own products of the marketplace in the product carousel.
  • Admin can add any product in the product carousel.
  • Admin can configure whether to allow/disallow product carousel for each position from the backend.

Manage Ad block orders easily in Odoo

  • Admin can view all the advertisement orders at one place.
  • Admin can select the maximum number of products to be shown the product carousel according to ad block position.

Approval Notification to Customer/Seller

  • A mail will be sent to the customer and seller on the approval of block content.

Easy Management for Ad blocks

  • Admin can publish/unpublish the advertisements displayed on the website.
  • Admin can view the list of customers and sellers who have purchased the advertisement blocks.
  • Admin can approve/deny or delete the advertisement products added by the sellers
  • Admin View
  • Seller View
  • Customer View

Globally Allow/dis-allow auto approval of Ad Blocks

Edit the name and price of Ad blocks; also set their Availability

Confirm the Sale Order of Ad block

Approve/Disapprove Ad Block Content

Seller can buy any Ad block as per the availability

Seller can put the banner for advertisement

Seller can put the products on advertisement

Customer can buy any Ad block as per the availability

Once order is confirmed; Customer can put the banner for advertisement
