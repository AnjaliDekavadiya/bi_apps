Website Subscription Management

Recurring revenue from Subscription in Odoo!

Website Subscription Management permits customers to pick subscription-based products and services directly from the Odoo website. Further, it saves you from the tedious job of managing all the product subscriptions by yourselves. Customers can view, renew, and cancel subscription plans from the website.

Why Do you Need Website Subscription Management?
Website Subscription Management permits you to offer products on subscription to customers. Further, the subscription model allows reliable recurring revenue, helps increase customer loyalty, and builds a healthy customer base.
The subscription model attracts more customers, reduces acquisition costs, and helps predict accurate business revenue. Website subscription management allows customers to view, renew, and cancel their subscriptions from their account page. Also, you can add the subscription details on the product page.
Also, you can create different subscription plans with various periods in the Odoo backend. And a single product can have multiple options to choose from.
Benefits of Website Subscription Management
  • The subscription management module permits reliable recurring revenue to ensure long-term income.
  • With the subscription model, you can build a long-term relationship with customers and focus on the subscribers. It creates a sense of community among customers.
  • Subscriptions permit Customer Lifetime Value to establish recurring revenue. Moreover, LTV offers a subscription to customers and multiple sales.
  • Subscription management creates an environment where customers are happy to interact with customers to increase engagement.
  • The subscription model authorizes better cash flow and inventory management as you get regular income from it.

Detailed Features List

Offer Subscription-based Products/Services on the Odoo Website

  • Website Subscription Management facilitates you to add subscription-based products/services on the Odoo website.
  • The Odoo admin can create and publish subscription-based products/services on the website.

Display Subscription Details on the Product Page

  • The website's product page shows the details of the available subscription plans.
  • It allows customers to choose the desired plan of a product/service and place order for the same from the Odoo website.

Start the Subscription After Order Confirmation

  • The subscription plan becomes active after the order confirmation.
  • Customers can view the list of all the purchased subscription plan(s) (Current and previous) in their Odoo Website Account.

Allows Customers to Manage Subscription Plan

  • The subscription model authorizes the customers to renew or cancel a subscription plan directly from their account.
